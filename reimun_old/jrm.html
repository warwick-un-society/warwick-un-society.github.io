Junior ReiMUN

In 2011 we introduced a new ‘Junior ReiMUN’ conference on the final Saturday in June.  This conference is aimed at first-time delegates or those with less than a year’s experience, and includes training and coaching in debating and resolution-writing skills, as well as the opportunity to practise these skills.  The conference also aims to train advisors in how to set up an MUN club in their school, how to coach their students to prepare for conferences and how to start their own conference.  Around twenty countries are represented, and there are three committees.  Following the success of our first Junior ReiMUN conference, we hope to make this a regular annual event.
