Headed notepaper

Many delegates find it useful to take notepaper to a conference.  This enables them to communicate with the chair or with another delegate during debate (eg. to suggest an amendment to the resolution under discussion, or to request another country’s support for your position).

The document below contains some example notepaper that you may use as a template if you wish.  It is for Afghanistan, but can be easily modified for another country.
