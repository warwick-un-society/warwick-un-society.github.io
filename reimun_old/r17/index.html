Checklist for advisors:

  • Please ensure that you are bringing delegates for the correct four committees (five if you have an SC seat).  As the conference is full it will not be possible for a country representative to sit in a committee to which they have not been assigned.  The country allocation list can be viewed here.
  • Please advise us of delegate and advisor names not later than Friday 14th September.  These should be e-mailed to applications@reimun.org. The delegates’ form can be downloaded here.
  • Please arrange payment of £20 per delegate by Friday 14th September.

ReiMUN 17  PROGRAMME

SATURDAY 6th OCTOBER
9.00 – 9.55      Registration and lobbying in committee rooms
10.00 – 10.20  Opening ceremony
10.25 – 3.25    Committees in session:
Session 1 will be focused on resolution construction, including policy statements
Session 2 will follow standard debate procedure and include two debates (resolutions to be prepared in advance)
12.15 – 1.30    Staggered lunch while committees are in session
3.25 – 3.45      Break
3.45 – 5.00      General Assembly two debates (including emergency)
5.00 – 5.20      Closing ceremony

Committee Agenda

Please note:
Topic 1 in Committees 1-5 will involve resolution construction on the day, so delegates should bring policy statements (which will be read out) but not resolutions.  (See Delegates’ Preparation Guide for more detail on the Resolution Construction procedure.)  Topic 2 will be the more familiar standard debate, and resolutions should be prepared in advance.  (Security Council will follow standard debate procedure for all topics.)

Committee 1 – Political
Topic 1 The question of water security
Topic 2 The question of the status of the Saharawi Arab Democratic Republic (Western Sahara)

Committee 2 – Human Rights
Topic 1 The question of indigenous people’s right to self determination
Topic 2 The question of bringing war criminals to justice

Committee 3 – Economic and Social
Topic 1 The question of international regulation of the internet
Topic 2 The question of provision of primary education

Committee 4 – Ecology and Environment
Topic 1 The question of endangered species
Topic 2 The question of fracking

Committee 5 - Health
Topic 1 The question of stem cell research
Topic 2 The question of waterborne diseases

Security Council
Topic 1 The question of the situation in Sudan
Topic 2 The question of the security situation regarding the Democratic Republic of Congo
