Unfortunately, ReiMUN will not be able to happen in 2013. We hope that it will return in 2014.
Junior ReiMUN will be happening in 2013.

We host our main conference in the autumn term.  The precise date varies from year to year, but always falls on a Saturday between September 20th and October 14th.
 
This is a one-day conference, with four or five committees plus a Security Council.  Around sixty countries are represented. 

Delegations are of four students (five for SC countries).  This conference attracts many experienced delegates, but is also accessible to inexperienced and first-time delegates. 

This conference is now in its 18th year.
